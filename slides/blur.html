backdrop-filter

  • Modifies whatever's behind an element, not the element itself
  • Needs to be somewhat transparent or you don't see anything
  • No need to use old-school hacks to achieve blurring background

filter

  • Affects the element itself, including its content
  • used for applying greyscale or constrast effects
